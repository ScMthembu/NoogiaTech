Our Background

NoogiaTech was found in the year 2017 and registered under CIPC in 2018.
NoogiaTech is a provider of expert IT support & services to businesses. NoogiaTech has served an ever-growing number of clients since its founding. In a relatively short period of time, What’s more, NoogiaTech has completed several comparable projects and the past IT projects demonstrate our ability to perform quality work on IT projects.

NoogiaTech is headquartered in Queensburgh. We are currently a Home & web-based company only; At this time, to keep the costs down, the company runs from an office at Home.

Our background, along with our knowledge of Computer Technology, have allowed us to come up and attain a strong foundation of technical skills.

Our Mission

Our mission is to become a recognizable as South African IT Support and Services Company, utilizing the connective power of the Internet to become a leader in the unique Business product market. We will offer start-ups, home-based businesses, and small to mid-sized companies the opportunity to own a unique, high quality professional services and products at an affordable prices than our competitors charge. 

Further, we plan to deliver an environment that creates trust in NoogiaTech. As a web resource as well as a huge marketplace where businesses can promote their own products...

Our Purpose

Our purpose is to spread out the word to the universe that we provide the best quality service and affordable to all.

We have a purpose of combining high quality products and cost-effective solutions along with effective and targeted marketing, while creating alliances with similar supporting products to give our customers and users an all-around company for their needs. As well as offering web resources such as a place to promote their businesses.

We aim to enhance the Information Technology field to a great and more sustainable resource to help the present meet the future sooner rather than later.
